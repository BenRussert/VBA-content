# XlSizeRepresents Enumeration (Excel)

Specifies what the bubble size represents on a bubble chart.



|**Name**|**Value**|**Description**|
|:-----|:-----|:-----|
| **xlSizeIsArea**|1|Area of the bubble.|
| **xlSizeIsWidth**|2|Width of the bubble.|
