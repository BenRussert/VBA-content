# View Methods (Word)

## Methods



|**Name**|**Description**|
|:-----|:-----|
|[CollapseAllHeadings](view-collapseallheadings-method-word.md)|Collapses all the headings in the document.|
|[CollapseOutline](view-collapseoutline-method-word.md)|Collapses the text under the selection or the specified range by one heading level.|
|[ExpandAllHeadings](view-expandallheadings-method-word.md)|Expands all the headings in the document.|
|[ExpandOutline](view-expandoutline-method-word.md)|Expands the text under the selection by one heading level.|
|[ForceLowresUpdate](http://msdn.microsoft.com/library/85f017eb-8506-53ad-d9f8-beb759572cde%28Office.15%29.aspx)||
|[ForceOffscreenUpdate](http://msdn.microsoft.com/library/d1394841-4cd2-0e3f-b4be-116baf1110b3%28Office.15%29.aspx)||
|[NextHeaderFooter](view-nextheaderfooter-method-word.md)|Moves to the next header or footer, depending on whether a header or footer is displayed in the view.|
|[PreviousHeaderFooter](view-previousheaderfooter-method-word.md)|Moves to the previous header or footer, depending on whether a header or footer is displayed in the view.|
|[ShowAllHeadings](view-showallheadings-method-word.md)|Switches between showing all text (headings and body text) and showing only headings.|
|[ShowHeading](view-showheading-method-word.md)|Shows all headings up to the specified heading level and hides subordinate headings and body text.|
