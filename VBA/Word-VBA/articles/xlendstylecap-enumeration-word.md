# XlEndStyleCap Enumeration (Word)

Specifies the end style for error bars.



|**Name**|**Value**|**Description**|
|:-----|:-----|:-----|
| **xlCap**|1|Caps are applied.|
| **xlNoCap**|2|No caps are applied.|
